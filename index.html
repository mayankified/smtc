SEMICONDUCTOR MANUFACTURING AND TESTING CENTER

The Semiconductor Manufacturing and Testing Center (SMTC) is dedicated to developing semiconductor-based devices across a wide spectrum of applications, including energy-harvesting: Photovoltaic Devices, Energy Storage Devices: Super Capacitors, Fuel Cells, Photoelectrochemical Cells, Sensors, Quantum Technologies, Memory Devices, but not limited to the above-mentioned areas only. The center covers all the aspects, including device fabrication, characterization, and modeling, with three labs:

‣ Semiconductor Technology (SemTec) Laboratory
‣ Analysis and Testing (AnTes) Laboratory
‣ Design and Simulation (DeSim) Laboratory
