Ph.D. Opportunities

Group welcomes candidates for Part-time as well as Full-time Ph.D. Engineering (BTech., MTech.) and from Science (Bsc., MSc.) for research activities with diverse expertise on various projects.

Full-time Ph.D.: UGC/CSIR/DST Fellowship

Students who have fellowships from UGC, CSIR, or DST can join the group and carry out research. Besides, those students who do not hold any fellowship are also welcome. They are guided to fill various scholarships during their stay. They can be also be enrolled after they pass through the recruitment process at SVNIT Surat.

Full-time Ph.D.: SVNIT Surat Fellowship.

For Postgraduates (M.Tech., M.Sc.)

Graduates who are currently pursuing Mtech or Msc are welcomed to the group for research as well as internship opportunities. Also, students who would like to work for MTech or Msc projects, are encouraged to be part of the group.

 

Interested candidates are requested to please drop a mail with their CV.
