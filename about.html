Semiconductor Technology (SemTec) Laboratory

The Semiconductor Technology (SemTec) Laboratory at SVNIT, Surat, is a hub of cutting-edge research and innovation in semiconductor manufacturing. The lab is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and tools that enable students and researchers to explore the intricate processes involved in semiconductor fabrication. Researchers at SemTec delve into the latest technologies and materials used in semiconductor manufacturing, aiming to enhance the efficiency, performance, and reliability of semiconductor devices. The lab offers hands-on experience in various semiconductor fabrication techniques, allowing students to gain valuable insights and practical skills. SemTec collaborates with industry partners and academic institutions to stay at the forefront of semiconductor technology. Research projects undertaken at the lab focus on developing new materials, processes, and technologies that drive advancements in the semiconductor industry. Overall, the Semiconductor Technology (SemTec) Laboratory at SVNIT, Surat, plays a crucial role in shaping the future of semiconductor technology through its innovative research and hands-on learning opportunities.

Analysis & Testing (AnTes) Laboratory

The Analysis & Testing (AnTes) Laboratory at SVNIT, Surat, is a state-of-the-art facility equipped with advanced instruments for the detailed analysis and testing of semiconductor materials. Our laboratory houses tools such as Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM), Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEM), and Energy-Dispersive X-ray Spectroscopy (EDS) systems, enabling high-resolution imaging and elemental analysis of semiconductor samples. We also offer services such as X-ray Diffraction (XRD) for crystallographic analysis and Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM) for surface imaging at the nanoscale. With a focus on providing comprehensive characterization capabilities, our laboratory serves as a vital resource for researchers and industry partners seeking to understand and optimize semiconductor materials for various applications.

Design and Simulation Laboratory

The Design and Simulation Laboratory at SVNIT, Surat, is a center for semiconductor design and simulation. It offers industry-standard software tools like Cadence, Synopsys, and Mentor Graphics for circuit design. Students can use hardware description languages such as Verilog and VHDL and access FPGA development boards for prototyping. The lab also provides Finite Element Analysis (FEA) and Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) software for analyzing mechanical and thermal behavior. With Electronic Design Automation (EDA) tools like Multisim and SPICE, students can design and analyze circuits. The lab's high-performance computing (HPC) cluster supports complex simulations. Overall, the lab provides a conducive environment for learning and research in semiconductor design.
