​Activities

2023-2024

Summer School on Semiconductor Technology

SVNIT Surat is launching a Summer school on semiconductor technology for UG/PG students fascinated by semiconductor manufacturing and packaging. Eligibility: B.Tech & B.E (3rd and 4th)/M.Tech/ME/MSc. Contact us for more details. July 8 - 19, 2024. Experts from IITs, NITs, and Industry will provide insights into semiconductor manufacturing, testing, packaging, process and device simulation for 10 days (2 Weeks).

IEEE Workshop on Electrochemistry

The IEEE SVNIT Student Chapter organized a comprehensive workshop on Electrochemistry Applications in Industry, held on 3rd February 2024 at SVNIT, Surat. The event featured Dr. Ramesh, an esteemed expert with over 32 years of experience in electrochemistry, who provided in-depth insights into the practical applications of electrochemical processes in various industrial sectors.
